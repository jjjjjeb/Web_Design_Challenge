Latitude vs. Humidity


City data was fed into a scatter plot with y for air saturation percentage and x displaying the latitude.

Results here suggest that humidity seems to rise with higher latitudes however this is likely due to higher seas to land ratios. Unfortunately no final correlation can be made and more research is needed.
